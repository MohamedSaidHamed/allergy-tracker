Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy is important to us. Allergy Tracker is designed to work with the minimum data necessary. Most data never leaves your device.

1. What Data We Collect

Allergy Tracker collects only what is strictly necessary to provide its core features:

  • Location (GPS coordinates): Used to fetch local pollen and weather data. Stored encrypted on your device via the platform's secure storage.
  • Symptom logs: Symptoms, generic medication categories, and severity ratings you choose to record. Stored locally on your device only — never transmitted.
  • Allergen preferences: Your selected allergens of interest. Stored locally on your device only.

2. What Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • No account registration required
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting services
  • No advertising identifiers

3. Third-Party Services

To display pollen and weather information, the app sends your approximate GPS coordinates to the following services. No personal identifiers are attached.

  • Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) — pollen and weather data. Free, open-source API. No account required.
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim (nominatim.openstreetmap.org) — location name lookup when you search manually. No account required.
  • CartoDB / CARTO (carto.com) — map tiles displayed in the app. Only tile requests are made; no personal data is sent.

These services receive only coordinates, not your identity. Please review their respective privacy policies for how they handle request data.

4. Background Location

If you grant background location permission, the app periodically checks your location (at most every 15 minutes or after moving 1 km) to send timely pollen spike alerts. This is only active if you explicitly allow it. You can revoke this permission at any time via your device settings.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Location data is stored using the platform's encrypted secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences).
  • Symptom logs and preferences are stored in local app storage and are removed when you uninstall the app.
  • No data is stored on any server operated by Allergy Tracker.

6. Your Rights

Because all personal data stays on your device, you are in full control:

  • Access & deletion: You can delete individual symptom logs from within the app, or delete all data by uninstalling the app.
  • Location: Revoke location permissions at any time in your device settings.

7. Children

Allergy Tracker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
